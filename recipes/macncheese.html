Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a home classic, that many families eat as a delicacy, or in a crunch. Mac and cheese can be cheap and it can be expensive.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flower
  • onion powder
  • Whole milk
  • Hot sauce
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni until tender.
  2. Place butter, flour, and onion powder in a pot. Whisk and heat over medium until butter is melted and mixture begins bubbling. whisk and cook for one minute after.
  3. Whisk the milk. Continue to cook and whisk over medium heat until milk begins to simmer. At this point the milk will be thick like gravy.
  4. Turn burner off. Season white sauce with salt and hot sauce.
  5. Begin to add shredded cheddar, one handful at a time. Whisking it until completely melted before adding more. Continue until all the cheese is melted into the sauce.
  6. Add the cooked and drained pasta to the pot with the cheese sauce, and stir to combine.
