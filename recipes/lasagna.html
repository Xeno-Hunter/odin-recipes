Lasagna

Description

Garfields favorite food. One of the oldest types of pasta made in wide flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Egg
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pasta sauce
  • Water
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown meat in large skillet on medium-high heat. Meanwhile, combine egg, ricotta, parsley, mozarella, and Parmesan.
  3. Drain meat; return to skillet. Stir in pasta sauce. Add water to empty sauce jar; cover with lid and shake well. Add to meat mixture; stir.
  4. Spread meat sauce into bottom of baking dish. top with layers of 3 lasagna noodles, add ricotta mixture, and the remaining meat sauce. Repeat layers twice. top with remaining noodles, meat sauce and cheeses. Cover with foil sprayed with cooking spray.
  5. Bake 1 hour or until heated through. Remove foil after 45 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before cutting.
